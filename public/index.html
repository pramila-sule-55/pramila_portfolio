SmartGas Guardian

An IoT-based safety solution with urban design aesthetics

IoT Safety Hardware

Introduction

SmartGas Guardian is a life-saving IoT device designed for home and industrial safety with an urban, street-inspired design language. It continuously monitors for dangerous levels of combustible gases and rapid temperature increases that may indicate a fire.

When a threat is detected, the system automatically shuts off the main gas supply valve and sends immediate alerts to homeowners via SMS and mobile app notifications. The project features a custom-designed enclosure with graffiti-inspired artwork, making safety technology visually appealing.
